About Us
We are a set of professionals who cater financial products to High Networth Individuals (HNI) clients, by ensuring adequate personal support & services for meeting their financial goals. We are AMFI registered distributors, having tie-up with leading mutual funds like HDFC, ICICI, DSP, SBI etc.

Mr. Anooj is highly skilled with his Post Graduation of MBA in Marketing and Finance from St. Joseph’s College, Bangalore.
